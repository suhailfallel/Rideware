Our Services & Solutions

We are a leading IT Software development company that offers cutting-edge solutions to businesses across diverse industry verticals.

Our Services & Solutions

1. Industrial process automation software solutions

We create design and implement software solutions to automate complex business solutions. We guarantee typically three functions: automating processes, centralizing information, and reducing the requirement for input from people.

2. Custom based software solutions

Custom based software i.e the process of designing, creating and deploying tailor-made software applications for specific users is one of our core functions. Custom software gives your company a way to be innovative and edge out your competitors which helps in increasing overall efficiency & creating a better overall user experience for your customers.

3. Assets Management

We develop dedicated Asset management software applications which is used to record and track an asset throughout its life cycle, from procurement to disposal. It provides an organization with information like where certain assets are located, who is using them, how they are being utilized and details about the asset.

4. Customized ERP & CRM solutions

Enterprise resource planning (ERP) software are also created by us that organizations use to manage day-to-day business activities such as accounting, procurement, project management, risk management and compliance, and supply chain operations. We develop most common types of ERP systems which include cloud ERP, on premise ERP, and hybrid ERP.

The CRM (Customer Relationship Management) helps companies to increase growth. It can boost sales and profit, but to fulfill increasing orders, one must have a stable ERP backbone. So basically you need both. A CRM system to drive sales and an ERP system that helps to streamline operations and reduce overall costs and both being customized is our speciality.

5. POS integration and sales

POS integration – an efficient connection between POS software and restaurant accounting and operations platform. With integration, your operations platform can pull detailed data directly from your POS in real time. POS API & Cloud-based POS systems are becoming increasingly popular which explains our software being high in demand. We also have eCommerce POS integration which is a digital solution to build a streamlined connection between your eCommerce platform and POS system.

6. Digital Marketing

Digital Marketing software includes tools that help businesses successfully execute campaigns, tap into their target markets and convert leads into sales. Companies, regardless of size, can benefit from this marketing process, allowing them to maximize their resources and get rid of repetitive tasks. Right from website development to choosing the right digital platform and implementing weekly updates on them which helps to maximise sales of the company is also our core activity.

7. Cloud based solutions

Cloud-based computing solutions (SaaS) which allows companies run on shared computing resources via the Internet and are maintained in remote data centers dedicated to hosting various applications on multiple platforms can be carried out through us. Using the right cloud providers like us can make a company to rapidly scale their business productivity software as their business grows or when a new company is added. By choosing us for your cloud based requirements we not only guarantee streamlined operations which will increase efficiency and profitability but also ensures 100% safeguarded security/compliance, uptime, and backups.

8. UI/UX Designs

We at RideWare generates software User interfaces for varied web solutions ranging from compound e-commerce systems & enterprise-level platforms to Cloud applications and web portals of various levels. We deliver easy to use user interface for Mobile, Web and windows application which are interactive, accessible, and adaptive hence creating an exceptional user experience on every screen.
